Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 11, 2026

1. Free Trial First

Every LeadSync plan starts with a 14-day free trial. No payment is taken until your trial ends, so you can evaluate the full platform before you are ever charged.

2. 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If LeadSync is not right for you, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on your first subscription payment. Contact us within 14 days of your first charge and we will refund it in full, no questions asked.

  • Applies to your first payment on monthly and annual plans
  • Request must be made within 14 days of the first charge
  • One-time setup or done-for-you service fees are refundable only if work has not yet started

3. Renewals and Partial Periods

  • Subscription renewals are not refundable once a new billing period has started
  • We do not provide pro-rata refunds for partial months or unused time
  • You can cancel at any time; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period and you keep access until then

4. How to Request a Refund

Email support@goleadsyncs.com from the email address on your account with the subject line "Refund Request". Please include your account email and the date of the charge.

Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method. Payments are processed by Paddle.com, our merchant of record, and refunds normally appear within 5 to 10 business days depending on your bank.

5. Billing Issues

If you believe you were charged in error (for example a duplicate charge), contact us before raising a dispute with your bank and we will resolve it quickly. You can also reach Paddle, our payment provider, directly via their buyer support at paddle.net.
